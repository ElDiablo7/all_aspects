# SEO Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Structure and Control
- Separate niches cleanly between paving and building
- Audit titles, metas, canonicals, indexability
- Finalise URL conventions
- Build service hubs

## Phase 2 — Top 30 Money Pages
Prioritise commercial pages for the strongest locations.
Target service + location combinations first.

## Phase 3 — Support Content
Add:
- pricing pages
- process pages
- FAQs
- planning / regulations pages where relevant
- maintenance / materials pages

## Phase 4 — Local Trust Expansion
Add:
- local job examples
- area references
- testimonials by service type
- stronger gallery and proof sections

## Phase 5 — Authority & Links
- optimise Google Business Profiles
- request review velocity
- gain local citations
- build quality local links

## Phase 6 — Scale Only After Validation
Scale only once:
- indexing is stable
- conversions are tracking
- key pages show movement

Do not spam out hundreds of weak pages before this is proven.
